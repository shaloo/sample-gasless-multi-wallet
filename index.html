Sample: Gasless Transactions in multi-wallet App!

This example demonstrates how a simple HTML/CSS/JS Web3 app can integrate with Auth and Arcana Gasless (Standalone) SDK to enable gasless transactions through Arcana wallet and MetaMask wallet. Parcel.

Use model:

For Arcana wallet, first initialize, connect and then use wallet to perform transactions. For MetaMask, log into MetaMask wallet first and then click init, getSCWAddress doTx.
